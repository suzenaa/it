CCSP – Legal, Risk & Compliance

The Legal, Risk, and Compliance module within the Certified Cloud Security Professional (CCSP) path focuses on responsibilities related to cloud-based governance and security oversight. This section covers essential policies, regulatory obligations, and risk strategies that align with cloud environments. These concepts are adapted from study materials available on igmGuru and aim to support professionals preparing for real-world cloud scenarios.

Essential Cloud-Based Tools

Tool Description
AWS Artifact Offers downloadable reports for compliance audits and certifications within AWS
Azure Compliance Manager Assists organizations in assessing and improving compliance with built-in regulatory templates
GCP Compliance Reports Delivers up-to-date compliance resources, including third-party verifications

Learning Tools and Frameworks

  • ISO 27001/27017 guidelines for cloud information security
  • Risk frameworks such as NIST RMF (Risk Management Framework)
  • Cloud Controls Matrix (CCM) by the Cloud Security Alliance
  • Compliance strategies for GDPR and HIPAA standards
  • Tools for privacy impact evaluation and data classification

Understanding Legal & Compliance Concepts

Professionals studying this domain gain a foundation in international privacy laws, data residency requirements, legal contracts in cloud service use, and investigation readiness. The CCSP also teaches methods for aligning internal governance with legal mandates, preparing for regulatory audits, and minimizing legal exposure across cloud service models.
